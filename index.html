About Us

& Our Work Process

ConsultED was founded in order to give education a facelift. Say goodbye to boring classrooms and replace them with an interactive learning experience.

Our Work Process.

Vision.

Our mission is to bring down the barriers brought by transferring skills and knowledge as they might hinder the ability of an institution to move full speed ahead.

Mission.

The pace at which the institution can progress is directly linked to how quickly their stakeholders are prepared. Be it a student, instructor, employee, customer, partner or a supplier. ConsultED is determined to turn this tedious task into an efficient and swift experience for all parties involved.

Why Us?

With ConsultED, you are not only betting on the success of your institution, but you are also empowering communities to thrive in difficult environments. As Lebanese Alternative Learning’s venture spin-off, ConsultED’s role is to ensure a continuous support to those communities.
